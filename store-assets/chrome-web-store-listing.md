## Extension Name (45 characters max)

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Organize all your ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini & Grok conversations. Switch AI models without losing context. Sync across devices.

## Detailed Description

**Omni - Your AI conversations, organized and portable.**

Stop losing context when switching between AI models. Omni automatically captures, organizes, and syncs all your conversations across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok.

### FEATURES

**Universal Capture**
Automatically capture conversations from:
- ChatGPT (GPT-4, GPT-4o, GPT-3.5)
- Claude (Claude 4, Claude 3.5, Claude 3)
- Gemini (Gemini Pro, Gemini Ultra)
- Grok (Grok-1.5, Grok-2)

**AI Model Switching**
Transfer your conversation context between any supported AI model with one click. Keep your train of thought when hopping between ChatGPT and Claude.

**Smart Organization**
- Create projects to organize conversations
- Add tags and notes for easy retrieval
- Powerful search across everything
- Timeline view of your AI interactions

**Integrations**
Connect your tools:
- GitHub - Sync repositories and issues
- Google Drive - Access files alongside conversations
- Notion - Export notes and summaries
- More integrations coming soon

**Cloud Sync & Backup**
- Sync across all your devices
- Automatic backups with version history
- Restore from any point

**Privacy First**
- Local-first architecture
- Your data stays on your device by default
- End-to-end encryption for cloud sync
- No tracking, no selling your data

## Privacy Practice Disclosures

**Websites Accessed:**
- chat.openai.com
- claude.ai
- gemini.google.com
- x.ai / grok.x.ai

**Data Collected:**
- Conversations (stored locally only)
- User preferences
- Usage statistics (opt-in, anonymized)

**Data Usage:**
- Data is used solely to provide the extension's functionality
- Conversations are stored locally on the user's device
- Cloud sync is optional and encrypted
- No data sold or shared with third parties

**Data Retention:**
- Users can delete all data at any time
- Local data persists until cleared
- Cloud data can be deleted from settings
